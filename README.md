# select-pardon-scarf
Project 4 for my Level 5 Diploma in Web Application Development at Dudley College of Technology (2025-2026)
Hi, I'm John E. Parman! You can get in touch with me through https://www.github.com/QualityLemons

Select-Pardon-Scarf is the code-name for a 'practice enterprise' for NEET (not in education, employment, or training) adults to learn programmable logic controller engineering through an authentic, role-based professional simulation. 
I came up with this idea through my work as a membership manager at BCIC West Midlands, an industrial cluster. We found that around 60% of respondent employers faced difficulties sourcing employees with PLC engineering training. This web app aims to provide a place for self-directed learning that is accessible to a wide variety of users, including those training into employment, employed individuals seeking further professional development, as well as employers.
