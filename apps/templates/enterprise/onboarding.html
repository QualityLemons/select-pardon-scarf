Employee Handbook: Safety First

In our facility, the following rules are non-negotiable:

  • Always Check Voltage: Use the multimeter before touching any circuit.
  • Lockout/Tagout: Never perform logic edits while a machine is active.
  • Report Faults: If a sensor is faulty, log it in the maintenance portal immediately.
